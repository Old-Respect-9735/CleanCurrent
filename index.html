Mission Statement

To Reduce Human Trash, Especially Recyclable Plastics, in Oceanic Ecosystems

CleanCurrent


It is common knowledge that trash in our oceans is a big problem. The damage to wildlife and coastal communities is dangerously high. Although completely removing the trash in our oceans is a complicated problem that cannot be solved at once, WasteWise eliminates a significant portion. With its highly accurate AI model, CleanCurrent will ensure that no more recyclable waste will make it into the ocean.
