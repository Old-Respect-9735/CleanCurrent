The Problem

Generally, people don't know what's recyclable and what isn't. Even for adults, throwing waste away often results in human error. The average human produces around 6 pounds of trash and recycle per day. A large chunk of plastics with recyclable potential end up in the landfill and these landfills often leak into the ocean. These harmful plastics that end up in the ocean spread out due to ocean currents and pollute a variety of ecosystems. This was shown by studies from a variety of marine biologists showing harmful amounts of microplastics in canned fish. These microplastics roaming through oceanic ecosystems and lifeforms have an effect on humans as well. Since coastal communities often rely on oceanic resources, trash and other pollution would damage the quality of life. This is where WasteWise comes in.
