Mini Game

Learn essential recycling knowledge while playing an engaging game

Rubble Rush

This small mini-game is about a player trying to prevent trash from getting into the recycling bin. Non-recycling items such as soggy cardboard and cermaic decrease your player's health and gettting the correct items increase your health. There are also powerups to make your game more interest!
